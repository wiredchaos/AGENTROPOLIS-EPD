# GET MONEY NEWS

**Status:** Flagship publication of AGENTROPOLIS-EPD  
**Domain:** Financial, business, tax, crypto, grants, and economic intelligence  
**Publishing model:** Verification-first, preview-first, confirmation-gated

## Position in the District

GET MONEY NEWS is the primary publication application housed inside AGENTROPOLIS-EPD. It consumes district Skills and publishing infrastructure but does not define the district boundary.

## Editorial Desks

- Markets and macroeconomics
- Crypto and digital assets
- Taxes and business operations
- Grants, capital, and funding
- Agentic finance
- Economic infrastructure
- Technology affecting money, ownership, and execution

## Required Publication Record

Every published item should preserve:

- Source list and retrieval dates
- Authoring and revision agents
- Human reviewer and approval state
- Draft and publication timestamps
- Categories, tags, and SEO metadata
- Media provenance and alt text
- Destination URL and platform identifier
- Publishing receipt identifier

## Release Gate

GET MONEY NEWS content may be researched and drafted autonomously. Scheduling, publication, destructive edits, and material revisions require explicit human confirmation.
