# District Architecture

## Identity

AGENTROPOLIS-EPD is the Editorial Publishing District of the AGENTROPOLIS Intelligence Grid.

It operates at Layer 2 as a district institution. It owns editorial governance, publishing orchestration, media handling, distribution controls, and publication receipts.

## District Boundary

AGENTROPOLIS-EPD is not a single publication or CMS plugin. It is the institution that governs how publications and publishing tools consume Skills from the Grid.

GET MONEY NEWS is the flagship application inside the district.

## Corridor

```text
Identity → Mandate → Research → Verify → Draft → Review → Approve → Publish → Receipt → Audit
```

No content may move from preview to scheduled or published state without explicit authorization.

## Governance

Every Skill is a contract with the Grid. Every publishing action must be attributable to an identity, mandate, approval event, and resulting receipt.
