# AGENTROPOLIS-EPD

**Editorial Publishing District of the AGENTROPOLIS Intelligence Grid.**

AGENTROPOLIS-EPD governs verified content creation, editorial review, approval-gated publishing, WordPress distribution, media operations, and auditable publication workflows.

## Flagship Publication

### GET MONEY NEWS

GET MONEY NEWS is the flagship financial and economic intelligence publication housed inside AGENTROPOLIS-EPD.

Primary coverage domains:

- Markets and macroeconomics
- Crypto and digital assets
- Taxes and business operations
- Grants, capital, and funding
- Agentic finance
- Economic infrastructure
- Technology affecting money, ownership, and execution

GET MONEY NEWS is an application of the district. It is not the district itself.

## District Role

**Domain:** Editorial operations, publishing governance, content distribution  
**Layer:** District / Institution  
**Function:** Convert verified intelligence into governed, platform-ready media.

## Core District Skills

| Skill | Role |
|---|---|
| `EPD-SCOUT` | Finds stories, filings, grants, market signals, and source material |
| `EPD-VERIFY` | Confirms claims, dates, citations, quotations, and provenance |
| `EPD-DESK` | Converts verified research into editorial drafts |
| `EPD-SEO` | Produces titles, descriptions, schema, categories, and tags |
| `EPD-MEDIA` | Manages images, captions, alt text, and media assets |
| `EPD-PREVIEW` | Generates the final publication preview |
| `EPD-GATE` | Blocks scheduling or publishing without explicit authorization |
| `EPD-PUBLISH` | Publishes approved content to configured platforms |
| `EPD-WIRE` | Distributes published stories through approved channels |
| `EPD-RECEIPT` | Records sources, revisions, approvals, and publication metadata |

## Security Principles

- Never store production credentials in the repository.
- Use scoped permissions and revocable credentials.
- Require preview and explicit confirmation for publication actions.
- Preserve source provenance and approval metadata.
- Separate unpublished investigations from public editorial infrastructure.
